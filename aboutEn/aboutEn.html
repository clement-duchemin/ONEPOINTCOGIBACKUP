About

The Cogitum website was created as part of the scientific research conducted by Nina Franiatte, Alexandra Delmas and Wim De Neys, affiliated with the Laboratoire de Psychologie du Développement et de l'Education de l'enfant (LaPsyDÉ, UMR CNRS 8240, Université Paris Cité) and the Sud-Ouest Research & Development department of the company onepoint.

Our main objective is to investigate the effectiveness of short videos in explaining various cognitive biases and enhancing logical reasoning.

The Cogitum website was developed by Clément Duchemin and Jérôme Fillioux, using onepoint's technical resources. Illustrations and graphic design were created by Eugénie Saget. The videos are inspired by the research work of Esther Boissin (see Boissin et al., 2021, 2022 for more details) and were created by Toscane Rabearisora and Gabin Carrier. Kaitlyn Cristelli provided the English voice, and Nina Franiatte provided the French voice.
